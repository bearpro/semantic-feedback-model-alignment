# Framework Laptop 13 — normalized product specification fragment

The product is a Framework Laptop 13 configured as the 2025 DIY Edition with AMD Ryzen AI 300 Series processors. It is a 13.5-inch notebook computer designed to be customized, upgraded, repaired, and assembled by the user. The DIY Edition is built by the owner; the expected setup time is 10 to 20 minutes and the assembly difficulty is moderate.

The laptop has three processor options. The Ryzen AI 5 340 option has up to 4.8 GHz clock speed, 6 CPU cores, and 12 threads. The Ryzen AI 7 350 option has up to 5.0 GHz clock speed, 8 CPU cores, and 16 threads. The Ryzen AI 9 HX 370 option has up to 5.1 GHz clock speed, 12 CPU cores, and 24 threads.

The processor can operate in three power modes. Performance mode has 30 W sustained processor power and 35 W boost processor power. Balanced mode has 25 W sustained processor power and 33 W boost processor power. Efficiency mode has 15 W sustained processor power and 25 W boost processor power.

Integrated graphics depend on the processor option. Ryzen AI 5 340 uses AMD Radeon 840M graphics with up to 2.9 GHz graphics clock and 4 graphics cores. Ryzen AI 7 350 uses AMD Radeon 860M graphics with up to 3.0 GHz graphics clock and 8 graphics cores. Ryzen AI 9 HX 370 uses AMD Radeon 890M graphics with up to 2.9 GHz graphics clock and 16 graphics cores. The NPU capability is up to 50 TOPS.

The thermal system contains a 65 mm by 5.5 mm cooling fan, a single 10 mm heat pipe, and Honeywell PTM7958 phase-change thermal interface material.

The operating system is not included in the DIY Edition; the user brings their own operating system. Storage can be selected up to an 8 TB WD_BLACK NVMe SSD or supplied by the user. Memory can be selected up to 96 GB DDR5-5600 or supplied by the user. Wireless connectivity is provided by AMD RZ717 Wi-Fi 7.

The laptop weighs 1.3 kg. Its physical dimensions are 15.85 mm thickness, 296.63 mm width, and 228.98 mm depth.

There are two display options. The Matte Display is 13.5 inches, has a 3:2 aspect ratio, 2256 by 1504 resolution, more than 400 nits brightness, 60 Hz refresh rate, 100% sRGB color gamut, and a matte anti-glare surface. The 2.8K Display is 13.5 inches, has a 3:2 aspect ratio, 2880 by 1920 resolution, more than 500 nits brightness, 120 Hz refresh rate, 100% sRGB color gamut, and a matte anti-glare surface.

The port system contains four user-selectable Expansion Card slots and one 3.5 mm combo headphone jack. The package includes the Framework Laptop 13, a power adapter, customizable Expansion Cards, a Framework screwdriver, and regulatory documents.

The product is designed for repairability. Replacement parts are available through the Framework Marketplace.
